Purpose
A mobile app (with web access) designed to support real estate agents and their clients in organizing, booking, and tracking property viewings. Built primarily for mobile use so that everything can be accessed easily in hand, it ensures convenience, speed, and on-the-go collaboration. The system will provide login-based access for both agents and clients, collaborative tools, tour scheduling, mileage/time tracking, and a complete decision history.
